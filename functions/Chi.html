Chi( z )

The hyperbolic cosine integral of z in SageMath. Alias of cosh_integral. Defined by

\[ \operatorname{Chi} (z) = \gamma + \ln(z) + \int_0^z du \: \frac{ \cosh(u) - 1 }{ u } \]

where γ is euler_gamma.
