exp_integral_e1( z )

The exponential integral E1 of z in SageMath. Defined by

\[ E_1 (z) = \int_z^\infty du \: \frac{ e^{ -u } }{ u } \]

Relation to exp_integral_ei for x > 0 :

\[ E_1 (x) = - \mathrm{Ei}(-x) \]
